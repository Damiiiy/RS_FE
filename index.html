Structure of the Bootcamp

Foundations:

Get hands-on with the fundamentals and key tools of your chosen track, whether it's Frontend Development, UI/UX Design, Backend Development, or Graphic Design

Project Development:

Apply what you've learned by working on real-world projects that reflect the tasks you'll face in the industry. These projects are designed to enhance your portfolio.

Industry Mentorship:

Gain personalized guidance from industry professionals who will provide insights, feedback, and career advice throughout your learning journey.

Core Skills Development:

Build on the foundational knowledge with more advanced techniques and tools, refining your skills and preparing for real-world challenges.

Capstone Project:

Create a final, portfolio-worthy project that demonstrates your proficiency and showcases your technical and creative abilities to potential employers.

Job Placement Assistance:

Receive tailored support for job search success, including resume building, portfolio development, and interview preparation to ensure you're ready for the next step in your career.

Services

At Rhedge Studio, we offer internships that provide hands-on experience in key areas of design and development. As an intern, you'll work alongside professionals to gain valuable skills and contribute to real-world projects

Logo Design & Branding

As an intern in our design team, you'll have the opportunity to assist in creating logos and branding strategies. You’ll learn how to capture the essence of a brand and help develop unique visual identities.

Frontend Development

Join our frontend team and work on creating responsive, visually appealing user interfaces. As a frontend intern, you'll learn to build websites and web applications using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, ensuring a smooth and engaging user experience.

Backend Development

As a backend intern, you'll dive into server-side development. You’ll work with databases, APIs, and server logic, learning how to build the infrastructure that powers web applications, ensuring everything runs smoothly and efficiently.

UI/UX Design

As a UI/UX intern, you’ll assist in designing user-friendly interfaces and improving user experiences. You’ll work on research, wireframes, and prototypes to help create seamless and intuitive digital products
